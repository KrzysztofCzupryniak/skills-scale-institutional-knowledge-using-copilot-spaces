# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

## Overview
OctoAcme uses a customer-first, iterative project management approach that balances clear planning with adaptive execution. The process is designed to deliver value quickly, keep stakeholders aligned, and continuously improve through regular reflection.

## Project Management Processes
- **Customer-first delivery**: Work prioritizes outcomes that matter to users and stakeholders.
- **Clear role ownership**: Defined responsibilities help keep work coordinated across teams and personas.
- **Lifecycle structure**: Projects follow a defined sequence of initiation, planning, execution, release, and retrospective.
- **Integrated risk and communication**: Risk management and stakeholder communication are part of every phase.
- **Continuous improvement**: Teams review progress and adapt through retrospectives and process updates.
